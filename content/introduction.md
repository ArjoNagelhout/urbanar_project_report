## MOET NOG WORDEN AANGEPAST
What would you change about your own environment? Most people have a strong opinion or feeling about where they live, where they find themselves regularly. Most people would also change certain things. Even if it is just an extra trash can or some more trees down the road. 

Currently, municipalities try to engage citizens by having them draw their views on paper. Not only is this difficult because people have to visualize the area, but it is rather boring. 

To help people voice their ideas, we invented an app that uses Augmented Reality to make urban design easier, more accessible, and most importantly, more fun. 

UrbanAR helps to make our cities a more collaborative effort, making it better for everyone. By giving citizens the possibility to provide solutions to urban planning problems by using their own insights and opinions, the government can more accurately build or renovate the city using the data from the app. UrbanAR directly shows citizens their design in the actual world. This makes for a more accurate design, and people can collaborate with each other by sharing their designs. 

Since Augmented Reality is a relatively new technology, we are positive that people will continue to use the app. Our user tests have shown that people would use the app if the municipalities were to ask them for input. Another thing that came out of the user tests is that it is so much fun. Therefore we are positive that UrbanAR will change the way urban planning is done. 

The design question was as follows:
>“How can we increase the quality and quantity of data collected on citizens’ opinions about urban planning and public space design efforts by municipalities using gamification and emerging technologies as location-based augmented reality?”
